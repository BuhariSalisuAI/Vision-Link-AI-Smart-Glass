# Vision-Link AI Smart Glass - User Manual
## Hausa Language Edition

---

### Barka da zuwa!

Vision-Link AI Smart Glass tana taimakawa marasa ganuwa wajen:
- Gano abubuwa a gaban ku
- Karatu rubutu daga alamomi da takardu
- Samun jagoranci ta hanya
- Gane mutane da ku sani
- Tuntuɓi iyali ta WhatsApp

---

## 1. Saita Kayan Aiki (Setup)

### Abubuwan da kuke bukata:
- Vision-Link AI Smart Glass
- Power bank (don caji)
- Rigar jiki (da ke ɗauke da kayan aiki)

### Matakin fara aiki:
1. **Caji na farko**: Caja kayan aiki na awa 3 kafin fara amfani
2. **Sanya rigar**: Sanya rigar jiki a jiki, saita kyamara ta fuskar ku
3. **Kunna**: Danna button na kunna na seconds 3
4. **Jira**: Ku ji "Barka da rana! Vision-Link AI tana shirye."

---

## 2. Umarnin Murya (Voice Commands)

### Magana da "Vision-Link" ko fara da:
- **"Abubuwa"** - Gano abubuwa a gaban ku
- **"Karatu"** - Karatu rubutu da kyamara ta gani
- **"Hanya"** - Samu jagoranci zuwa gida
- **"Wani ne"** - Gane mutumin da ke gaban ku
- **"Taimako"** - Aika saƙon gaggawa

### Misali:
Ku ce: **"Vision-Link, karatu"**
Zai ce: **"Ina karatu rubutu..."** sannan ya faɗa maku abin da ya gani.

---

## 3. Button Controls (Idan Murya Ba Ta Yi Ba)

| Button | Aiki |
|--------|------|
| Danna sau 1 | Karatu rubutu (OCR) |
| Danna sau 2 | Gano abubuwa |
| Danna tsaye (2 seconds) | Tuntuɓi gaggawa |
| Danna sau 3 | Jagoranci |

---

## 4. Jagoranci (Navigation)

### Samu hanya:
1. Ce: **"Vision-Link, hanya zuwa [wuri]"**
   - Misali: "Hanya zuwa kasuwa"
2. Zai ce: **"Na shirya hanya..."**
3. Ku bi umarnin murya:
   - "Koma hagu" = Turn left
   - "Koma dama" = Turn right
   - "Tafi kai tsaye" = Go straight

---

## 5. Gaggawa (Emergency)

### Idan akwai gaggawa:
1. Danna button na tsaye (2 seconds) KO
2. Ce: **"Vision-Link, taimako"**

Zai:
- Aika saƙo zuwa iyali ku ta WhatsApp
- Raba wurin ku (GPS location)
- Ce: **"An aika saƙon gaggawa. Taimako yana zuwa."**

---

## 6. WhatsApp Integration

### Iyali za su iya:
- Aika saƙo don neman status ku
- Samu wurin ku na yanzu
- Aika umarnin neman taimako

### Misalin saƙo:
- **"Status"** - Zai ba ku cikakken bayani
- **"Location"** - Zai raba GPS coordinates
- **"Help"** - Zai aika gaggawa
